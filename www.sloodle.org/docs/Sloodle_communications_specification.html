There are 2 types of communication involved with sloodle.

In-World

This covers any communications that take place only in-world, including chat and link-messages.

  • Communicating between scripts and/or prims - type and format of in-world messages
  • Sloodle channels - channel numbers for chat and link-messages


Second Life - Moodle

This covers all communications which take place between Sloodle objects in Second Life, and the Moodle site.

  • Communicating between server and client - structure and format of HTTP/XMLRPC messages
  • Sloodle status codes - numerical codes which may be sent/received, and what they mean
